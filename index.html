About Me

Brief Bio Dr. Vandana Singh is an academic leader with extensive experience in transdisciplinary program development and collaborative curriculum innovation. At the University of Tennessee, Knoxville (UTK), she leads the design and implementation of high-impact undergraduate and graduate programs that span multiple academic disciplines and align with rapidly evolving industry needs.

Dr. Singh’s leadership has resulted in the successful development and Tennessee Higher Education Commission (THEC) approval of five undergraduate degrees—Applied Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Innovative Transdisciplinary Studies (ITS), and Information Sciences —as well as two graduate programs in Sustainability and Data Science. The ITS program features a highly customizable, certificate-based curriculum created in collaboration with faculty from thirteen colleges across UTK. In total, Dr. Singh has facilitated the engagement of over 100 faculty members and a dozen university-wide committees, working closely with department heads, associate deans, and industry partners to advance CECS’s academic mission.

Prior to joining CECS, Dr. Singh spent over two decades conducting internationally recognized interdisciplinary research and preparing professionals in the fields of information science and technology. Her global research collaborations span computer science, engineering, nursing, education, business information systems, and the arts and sciences, and include participation in international teams with members from up to ten countries. Dr. Singh’s work reflects a career-long commitment to academic innovation, global engagement, and the power of collective effort.
